Star Wars: Force Awakens

Sean Miller

There has been an awakening, have you felt it? Star Wars is back! The third highest grossing movie of all time! With J.J. Abrams the director of the new Star Trek movies, Super 8 and more this movie is Star Wars which most everybody knows and enjoys. With Harrison Ford and some other actors from the classic films, this movie also brings in new actors which I think did a great job in this movie. The visuals were great with great imaging and conception put into the new locations and in other pieces of the movie like the costumes and all. It was awesome and made me feel like a was a little kid again, like the first time I watched Star Wars. A lot of people have been waiting for this day, and now it has come, the Force definitely has, awakened.

Owen Vandevelde

Last December, the world was blessed by another addition to the Star Wars collection of movies, and this time, the movie was really damn good. J.J. Abrams is an excellent director and was credited with many other visually stunning films. The flow of the movie was smooth and it was never boring. The casting for the new roles was also great. Rey, Daisy Ridley, did spectacular, and is a very good main character. The only disappointment the movie had was the development of Adam Driver’s character, Kylo Ren. He wasn’t that bad, and looked like how a Sith should look, from the outside. Under the mask is a confused boy in his mid 20’s struggling with an identity crisis. He doesn’t know if the path he has chosen is right. I believe that this movie brought Star Wars back with all its might and glory and I can’t wait for the next installment in the series.
