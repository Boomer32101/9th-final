Batman v Superman

Sean Miller

In Batman vs Superman: Dawn of Justice which came out a while ago, we saw Ben Affleck as Batman and Jesse Eisenberg as Lex Luthor. Though I have only seen a few other actors be Batman, and I have not seen any other movies with Ben Affleck in them, I think Ben Affleck did a pretty good job playing Batman, he was an older dude who had been Batman for quite sometime and just locking up criminals isn’t enough for him anymore. Though Ben Affleck pretty much just plays Batman the whole time, even without the mask he still portrays a kind of grouchy character and not smooth ladies man Bruce Wayne, he wasn’t bad. In Now You See Me Jesse Eisenberg did a great job playing a lead role, and in this film I don’t think he did as good as he did in Now You See Me, he had an interesting idea for Lex Luthor, making him almost more like the Joker than classic bald mastermind Lex Luthor. This movie is part of a set up for the Justice League movie which I think should be interesting because their have been quite a few animated Justice Leagues but not really any live action ones.Though it was long and a bit confusing in the addition that is like 30 minutes longer(Yaah!!!) I think it will be interesting to see what they took out. There are a lot of negative things I could say about this movie but also a great highpoint in this movie and even though it was only for a moment, there was Neil deGrasse Tyson which is enough for me to want to see this movie.

Owen Vandevelde

Okay, first of all, this movie wasn’t as bad as people say but it still was a wreck. It had so much potential to be a epic fight and show the formation of the Justice League but instead it was just a 2 and a half hour set up for the next movie, “Justice League”. It’s story made no sense, like why Batman was inclined to fight Superman, and why Batman didn’t kill Superman. Their mom had the same name! What the hell. Lex Luthor was basically the Joker, not the tech savvy mastermind in the comics. Speaking of comics, why was Doomsday there? I understand Lex’s intentions to create Doomsday but he never appeared in the original comic and really didn’t add to the plot. He just gave them a reason to stop fighting each other and fight together. Wonder Woman also didn’t add to the story either. The best part of the movie was getting to see Flash, Aquaman, and Cyborg or the vision of the future where Superman is the leader. Batman looked really cool in that desert outfit.
