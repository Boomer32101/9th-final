Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation

Sean Miller

Tom Cruise is back baby! And this time his secret agent squad doesn’t have the government for support. Tom Cruise is just cruisin by, this time he gets to go underwater. This movie is like other mission impossibles, it is a mission impossible to miss this one. It has got a cool motorcycle scene, a theatre scene, Tom Cruise has got the hots so a double agent, and Simon Pegg almost dies. Gotta find plans and awesome break-in. I mean if you haven’t seen Tom Cruise, you haven’t seen anything. This movie was good, Tom Cruise jumped on a plane which was his own stunt, he doesn’t need any stunt double because he’s got a turtleneck. Awesome trippy face mask scene. Sorry I didn’t remember much about this movie, but I looked up other critic reviews and they said the exact same thing about the underwater scene and how awesome it was.

Owen Vandevelde

The latest installment in the Mission: Impossible series is, in my opinion, the best. It is very exciting with many action/intense scenes. The movies plot revolves around a secret organization call Syndicate and Ethan Hunt trying to find them. One of the most exciting points in the movie is when Hunt has to switch a file underwater, and then go on a motorcycle chase right after. The movie keeps the attitude of the previous ones and I am waiting for the next.
